Vier is a multidisciplinary artist.
Their work spans animated short films, immersive experiences and art exhibitions.


Vier plays with light and shadow to create artworks that contain simultaneous imagery.

While exploring the narrative potential of these images, they conceive interactive worlds about perception, variation, language and queer history.
